<h3 data-typography="Name and description">Name and description</h3>

Provide a unique name and a short description for your application.<br />
This works similarly to naming a GitHub repository, the name helps you identify your app later, and the description gives context
about its purpose or functionality.

<h3 data-typography="Configuration">Configuration</h3>

Next, select whether your application is Business-to-Business (B2B)<br />
or Business-to-Consumer (B2C).

<div className="warning-message"> This selection cannot be changed once the application is created.</div>

##### Business to business

B2B apps connect with business entities only — they do not support end-user interactions.

##### Business to consumer

B2C apps are intended for individual consumers and include APIs for managing personal user data and sessions.

The chosen configuration determines which APIs and features your app can access. Choose carefully based on your target users and integration requirements.
